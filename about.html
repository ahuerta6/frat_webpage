About

The Alpha Phi Chapter of Pi Kappa Phi was founded in 1935 at Illinois Institute of Technology, located just south of downtown Chicago. Since its foundation, the chapter has played a critical role in instilling in its members a spirit of fraternity, academic excellence, and community involvement. The fraternity has actively contributed to the dynamic tapestry of campus life over the years, organizing charity events, leadership programs, and social activities. The IIT campus's location provides members with unique opportunity to engage with the diverse cultures and opportunities that Chicago has to offer. As the Alpha Phi Chapter evolves, it stays committed to sustaining Pi Kappa Phi's basic ideals, forming well-rounded individuals who are not just academically accomplished but also community-minded and socially responsible.

We stand as a testament to a cohesive brotherhood united in the unwavering pursuit of excellence. Within our chapter, we cultivate an environment that encourages each member to strive for their individual aspirations, recognizing that the collective strength of our brotherhood is built upon the diverse talents and ambitions of each individual. Whether it be in academics, leadership, or personal growth, the Alpha Phi Chapter is committed to providing a supportive network that empowers each brother to reach their fullest potential.
